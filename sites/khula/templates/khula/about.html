About Khula

Khula is a fashion magazine front end on microscopis: niche high-fashion houses, opinionated essays, and images pulled from open stock. Content is stored in the project database and seeded for demo purposes.

The layout and typography match the original Khula static demo: IBM Plex Mono navigation, Safari-flavored defaults, and the same asymmetric home grid and article rhythm.

Contact on the contact page is a layout placeholder only.
